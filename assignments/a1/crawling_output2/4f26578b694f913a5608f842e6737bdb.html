Preview is ODU's on-campus orientation program. The events include educational planning, course selection and registration, student and parent interaction, and an overview of resources and campus life.

Preview is different from an Open House, which is a general information session about the University for prospective students. If you are looking for information about Open House or a campus tour, please visit the Future Monarch Center webpage. If you are looking for information about on-campus housing and move-in, please visit the Housing webpage.

Am I required to attend Preview?

Locate Your 'Student Type' Below Understand Your Requirements
New high school graduate (attending classes on campus)

An on-campus Preview is required.
New high school graduate (distance learner/ODU online program)
 Contact your enrollment coordinator to schedule an appointment or if you have questions about your next steps.

3+ years since high school graduation and few or no transfer credits

 Online Orientation and an on-campus Preview are required.
Transfer student (1-23 credits) Online Orientation and an on-campus Preview are required.
Transfer student (24+ credits)

Online Orientation is required, and an on-campus Preview is strongly recommended. If you opt out of the on-campus Preview, please complete the Online Orientation and then make an appointment with your academic advisor for registration.

Please note: All students with majors within the Strome College of Business are required to attend the on-campus Preview.
Distance learner/ODU online program Online Orientation is required. Contact your program advisor to set up an advising appointment.

Second-degree or degree continuing student

 Make an appointment with your academic advisor to register for classes.

Non-degree student

 Register for classes in Leo Online. If you need assistance, make an appointment with your academic advisor. You will attend Preview once you become degree-seeking.
