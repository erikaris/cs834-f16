creator: Michael L. Nelson
title: CS 895 - Time on the Web
date: Fall 2010
description: This class will be a research seminar exploring the areas of Web resources evolve over time, how the resources are cached and archived, and how we can interact with and use archived Web resources. The class will consist of a variety of research papers that the students will read, present and lead discussion of during class. There will also be a semester long project that will demonstrate, measure or explore some of the research ideas presented in class.
