Communications Bootcamp

You have the skills and know-how to get the job done, but do you have the communication skills to effectively lead a team or to present before an audience? This three-day bootcamp for professionals on the executive path will give you the tools you need to communicate professionally in informal situations. Learn to build the relationships you need for professional growth

Learn More >

Pre-Accelerator

If you are on the journey of starting your own business, but do not yet have a viable product and no business plan, ODU's Pre-Accelerator can develop your company to the next level.

Learn More >

Big Data: Data Wrangling

This three-day bootcamp, we will cover aspects common to all Big Data investigations, including: defining Big Data, surveying tools and techniques for processing Big Data, and visualizing selected aspects of Big Data. The focus of the camp will be primarily on velocity and variety.

Learn More >

Project Management

A Project Management Professional (PMP) credential from the Project Management Institute is one of the most widely requested and recognized certifications in the world for project managers. Learn more about our Project Management offerings today!

Learn More >

Certified Financial Planner Program

The Executive Certificate in Financial Planning prepares individuals for success in the demanding but highly rewarding financial planning industry. Learn how you can get started today!

Learn More >

Graduate Certificate in Public Sector Leadership

Governmental organizations are facing unprecedented challenges requiring enterprising leadership skills. ODU’s Graduate Certificate in Public Sector Leadership was developed to equip public sector personnel with the tools needed to solve today’s management problems—such as project and resource management, financial administration, contracting principles, cyber security and information technology.

Learn More >

Classroom Management Boot Camp

The upcoming Classroom Management Boot Camp will use workshops, collaborative group activities, and state-of-the-art simulations to help educators improve their classroom management skills.
