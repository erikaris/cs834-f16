How and when you visit ODU is up to you.

Making it your best college visit is up to us!

Nothing beats a campus visit to determine if life and learning as a Monarch suits you. From daily, scheduled campus tours and information sessions to open houses, you'll have access to campus, our programs, and our staff. Plus our team of student guides is ready to share their Monarch pride and all the reasons why they love it here! We look forward to seeing you on campus! Registration is required for all events.

Our campus is so bike friendlly it was named a bronze-level winner of the Bicycle Friendly Universities Award - no. 7 in the Top 40 Most Bike Friendly Universities by Best College Values.

With the luxury of our location on the Elizabeth River, students enjoy plenty of green and tranquil spaces in the midst of our metropolitan setting.

Campus tours with an admissions' presentation are offered at scheduled times on weekdays and select Saturdays. As an attendee you'll hear an overview of the university, get a glimpse of campus life, learn about our academic programs, and tour the campus with a student guide - a true campus expert!. You should reserve approximately 2 hours to complete both the presentation and tour.

Registration is required and once submitted, a confirmation email with driving and parking directions as well as a parking pass (please print and bring with you the day of your visit) will be sent. Driving directions can be accessed here. If using a GPS, the university's street address is 5115 Hampton Boulevard, Norfolk, VA 23529.

Your visit will begin at the Future Monarch Center located at 1101 in Webb University Center.

Visit Campus

Group Tours are offered Monday through Friday throughout the fall, spring and summer.

Groups of 20 or more* students are asked to share their visit requirements via the "request online" link below. Once submitted, an admissions associate will contact your group's designated point person to make the proper arrangements and confirm your visit.

*In an effort to offer the best campus visit experience, we must limit groups to 50 people and for every 10 students, there must be one chaperone.

Groups of less than 20 are asked to contact the Future Monarch Center at 757-683-3648 to register.

Request Online

More indepth than our daily information sessions, open houses at ODU include a welcome by university leadership, a student Q&A panel, a campus resource fair with representatives from housing and residence life, financial aid, study abroad, career development services, etc. Also included are academic presentations by faculty, complemented by a campus walking tour and a visit to one or more residence halls. The event components are designed to help you discover, explore and engage with ODU to decide if the life and learning here feels right for you.

Registration is required and pertinent event information will be emailed to you upon registration. Driving directions can be accessed here. If using a GPS, the university's street address is 5115 Hampton Boulevard, Norfolk, VA 23529.

Please note: Due to space limitations, we are unable to accommodate group visits at our Open House events.
