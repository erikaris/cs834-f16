Academic Integrity

ODU and the Department of Computer Science take academic integrity seriously. See Monarch Citizenship for the ODU Honor Code and Honor Pledge.

See the Academic Integrity page for more information on what is considered cheating or plagiarism in the Department of Computer Science.

You are responsible for knowing the rules or asking for clarification.

Communication

Faculty and staff will communicate with you through your @cs.odu.edu email account. Check it regularly and respond promptly.

Tips for professional communication:

  • How to Email a Professor, Michael Leddy
  • How to Talk to a Professor, Michael Leddy
  • Practice general courtesy.
    • Be polite and professional in email.
    • Ask, do not demand.
    • Knock on the door before entering an office.
    • Give notice if you must miss class.
