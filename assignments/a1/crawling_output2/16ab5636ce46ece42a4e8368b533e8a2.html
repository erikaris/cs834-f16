At ODU we understand that as a transfer student, you have unique needs that require a wide array of campus resources. The Advising and Transfer Programs Office is here to assist you with your transition to the university. The services and programs offered to you as a Monarch are endless so take advantage of every opportunity. We encourage you to Explore, Experience, and Engage!

Even before you set foot on campus we encourage you to explore your transfer resources.

Once admitted to Old Dominion University the Advising and Transfer Programs Office will continue to provide assistance and support during your transition. Students who attend ODU as non degree are able to schedule an appointment with us to ensure they are enrolled in the proper courses for their program. We strive to help you identify services and resources that will assist you in achieving your academic, career and personal goals.
