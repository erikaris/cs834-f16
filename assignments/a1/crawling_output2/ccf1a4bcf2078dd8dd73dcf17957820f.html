  • Hampton Roads

Norfolk is just one of 16 cities that make up Hampton Roads, Virginia. Off campus, there is always something to do. All year round Monarchs enjoy life at the ocean front in Virginia Beach, the museums and sporting events in downtown Norfolk, and the historical attractions of Williamsburg and Busch Gardens. Hampton Roads hosts many major attractions for all to enjoy.

Go Shopping

  • MacArthur Center, Norfolk
  • Greenbrier Mall, Chesapeake
  • Prime Outlets, Williamsburg
  • Ghent business district, Norfolk

Go Eat

  • University Village, ODU Campus
  • Doumar's Barbeque, Norfolk
  • Baker's Crust, Norfolk
  • Ghent business district, Norfolk

See a Movie

  • Regal Cinemas at MacArthur Center, Norfolk
  • Cinema Café, Hampton Roads
  • Naro Expanded Cinema, Norfolk

Go Play

  • Ted Constant Convocation Center, ODU Campus

  • Busch Gardens, Williamsburg

Go Learn

  • Chrysler Museum of Art, Norfolk
  • Colonial Williamsburg, Williamsburg
  • Virginia Marine Science Center, Virginia Beach
  • National Maritime Center, Norfolk
