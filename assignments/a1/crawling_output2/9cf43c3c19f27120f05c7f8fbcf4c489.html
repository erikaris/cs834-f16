The ODU Monarch Family Experience

Thank you for your confidence in sending your "favorite" student to Old Dominion University.

The building blocks for student success are all here. ODU's strong tradition of educational quality and leadership, outstanding faculty and talented student body combine to create a university community that is both challenging and supportive.

Mission: Old Dominion University's Family Program strives to promote student success, learning, and development through parent and family member involvement within the University. ODU's Family Program connects parents to fellow parents, as well as faculty members and administrators, to encourage active engagement and support throughout the student's tenure at ODU.

Goals:

  • Provide timely information and resources to parents and family members through open communication, programs, and publications.
  • Emphasize and help parents and family members recognize the importance of their roles as mentors and coaches to their student(s).
  • Encourage parents and family members to empower their student(s) to become independent learners by helping parents and family members learn to navigate University resources.
