Welcome to Old Dominion University on iTunes U

iTunes U is a free service hosted by Apple where users download educational content. This content can be played on your computer, iPod, tablet, or other portable devices.

The Old Dominion University public iTunes U site allows us to share the outstanding work and research of ODU faculty, staff and students.

The private ODU iTunes site is only for the University community. This site may contain audio or video content related to specific courses or departments.

Getting Started

  • If you have not yet installed Apple's iTunes software, you will need to download and install this free software.
  • Need help with iTunes?
  • Do you have content to add to ODU on iTunes U, email itunesu@odu.edu
